Text Formatting

Superscript Tag

Superscript Tag is used to put text above the normal position.
Example: (a+b)2 = a2+b2+2ab. Here two is kept inside Superscript Tag.

Subscript Tag

Subscript Tag is used to put text bottom the normal position.
Example: H2O. Here two is kept inside ubscript Tag.

Strike through Tag

Strike through Tag is used to put horizontal line through some text. Mostly used as deleted or changed.
Example: The price of the product is $999 $899.
